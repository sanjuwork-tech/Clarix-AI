## Critical Context: Two Schemes, Two Different Products

**Old Scheme (8 papers)** — students registered before July 2023, still appearing under old syllabus.  
**New Scheme (6 papers)** — applicable from May 2024 onwards. Every new student is on this.

Your current form and report are built entirely on the **Old Scheme**. Since most active CA Final students (especially repeaters) are transitioning to or already on the New Scheme, this is a fundamental structural error — not a cosmetic one.

**Resolution: Add a scheme selector as the very first question. Show different paper structures based on the answer.**

## Part 1: Correct Paper Structure — Both Schemes

### New Scheme (ICAI 2023, effective May 2024) — 6 Papers / 600 Marks

| Paper | Full Official Name | Short Form | Group | Marks |
|-------|-------------------|------------|-------|-------|
| Paper 1 | Financial Reporting | FR | Group 1 | 100 |
| Paper 2 | Advanced Financial Management | AFM | Group 1 | 100 |
| Paper 3 | Advanced Auditing, Assurance and Professional Ethics | AAP | Group 1 | 100 |
| Paper 4 | Direct Tax Laws and International Taxation | DT | Group 2 | 100 |
| Paper 5 | Indirect Tax Laws | IDT | Group 2 | 100 |
| Paper 6 | Integrated Business Solutions | IBS | Group 2 | 100 |

**Paper 6 (IBS) — critical special notes:**
- Open-book examination
- Duration: 4 hours (not 3 hours like other papers)
- Structure: 5 case studies × 25 marks — attempt any 4
- Pattern: 40% MCQ + 60% descriptive
- No negative marking
- Covers: Strategic Management + multi-disciplinary integrated scenarios
- This paper has NO study material in the traditional sense — it's purely application

**Passing criteria — New Scheme:**
- Minimum 40% in each individual paper
- Minimum 50% aggregate in the group
- Exemption: if 60+ in a paper, permanently exempted from that paper in future attempts

### Error 5 — SA 540 hallucination (critical — will mislead students)
**Location:** Paper 3 (Audit) diagnosis row, ICAI chapter / standard column  
**Current (wrong):** `SA 540 – Auditing group financial statements`  
**Actual SA 540:** Standards on Auditing 540 = "Auditing Accounting Estimates, Including Fair Value Accounting Estimates, and Related Disclosures"  
**Group financial statements:** That is SA 600 = "Using the Work of Another Auditor"

**Fix — replace the SA 540 row in Audit diagnosis:**
```
REMOVE: SA 540 – Auditing group financial statements
ADD:    SA 540 – Auditing Accounting Estimates and Related Disclosures
        (Key areas: management's assumptions, fair value estimates, IT-generated data)
ADD:    SA 600 – Using the Work of Another Auditor (if group audit context is intended)
```

### Error 6 — SA 560 hallucination (critical)
**Location:** Paper 3 (Audit) diagnosis row  
**Current (wrong):** `SA 560 – Auditing internal controls`  
**Actual SA 560:** Standards on Auditing 560 = "Subsequent Events"  
**Internal controls:** SA 265 = "Communicating Deficiencies in Internal Control" or SA 315 = "Identifying and Assessing the Risks of Material Misstatement"

**Fix — replace the SA 560 row:**
```
REMOVE: SA 560 – Auditing internal controls
ADD:    SA 560 – Subsequent Events
        (Key areas: events between period-end and audit report date, 
        Type 1 vs Type 2 events, adjusting vs non-adjusting)
ADD:    SA 265 – Communicating Deficiencies in Internal Control
        (if internal controls context is intended)
```

### Error 7 — Ind AS 102 hallucination (critical — completely wrong standard)
**Location:** Paper 1 (FR) diagnosis row  
**Current (wrong):** `Ind AS 102 – Inventories (FIFO, LIFO, weighted-average)`  
**Actual Ind AS 102:** Ind AS 102 = "Share-based Payment" (stock options, ESOPs, equity-settled transactions)  
**Inventories standard:** That is Ind AS 2 = "Inventories"

**This is the most dangerous hallucination in the report.** If a student follows this, they will spend hours studying Share-based Payment thinking they are studying Inventories. Both are examinable but are entirely different topics.

**Fix — replace the Ind AS 102 row:**
```
REMOVE: Ind AS 102 – Inventories (FIFO, LIFO, weighted-average)
ADD:    Ind AS 2  – Inventories (FIFO, weighted-average cost methods, NRV write-downs)
ADD:    Ind AS 102 – Share-based Payment (if ESOP/stock options context is relevant)
```

## Appendix: New Scheme Exam Pattern Summary (for AI prompt context)

```
CA Final New Scheme — Exam Pattern (effective May 2024)

Papers 1-5 (FR, AFM, Audit, DT, IDT):
  - Duration: 3 hours
  - Total marks: 100 per paper
  - Pattern: 30% MCQ + 70% descriptive
  - No negative marking
  - Passing: 40% per paper minimum

Paper 6 (IBS — Integrated Business Solutions):
  - Duration: 4 hours
  - Total marks: 100
  - Format: Open-book examination
  - Structure: 5 case studies × 25 marks — attempt any 4
  - Pattern: 40% MCQ + 60% descriptive
  - No negative marking
  - Content: Multi-disciplinary integrated case studies (FR + AFM + Audit + Tax + Strategy)

Group passing criteria:
  - Group 1 (Papers 1, 2, 3): 40% per paper + 50% aggregate (min 150/300)
  - Group 2 (Papers 4, 5, 6): 40% per paper + 50% aggregate (min 150/300)

Exemption (New Scheme only):
  - Score 60+ in any paper = permanently exempted from that paper
  - Exemption is permanent, not attempt-limited
  - Student can voluntarily surrender exemption if desired
```
